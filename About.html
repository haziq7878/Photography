About

Hi There!

I'm Humza. The kid behind the camera.

I am currently studying Mechanical Engineering for my undergraduate degree. I always had a passion for photography. I'd walk outside my house just to admire the sky, appreciate the natural beauty around me like plants, flowers, the sun and then moon. Whenever I'd see the sunset, in that moment I would do just one thing, appreciate the moment and make it a memory. That is, by photographing it. This year I discovered yet another passion, namely digital art. I'm really keen to share my art with you.

Besides practicing photography and digital media, I can be found spending quality time with my friends, likely discussing robots or aliens.
